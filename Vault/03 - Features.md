# ✨ Core Features

CrowdVote AI isn't just a voting app; it's a **Democratic Intelligence Engine**. Here's what makes it special:

## 1. 🏗️ The Arena (Analytics Hub)
- **Live Swarm View**: See real-time consensus for all 140 constituencies.
- **Historical Context**: Compare 2026 predictions against 2021 actual results side-by-side.
- **Micro-Stats**: Track win margins and candidate lineups for every seat.

## 2. 🗳️ The Voting System
- **Alliance Mapping**: Your vote for a candidate automatically weights the LDF, UDF, or NDA fronts.
- **Confidence Weights**: Don't just vote—indicate how *sure* you are (1-10). High-confidence votes have more impact on the swarm.
- **User Rank Factor**: Votes from top-ranked "Oracles" carry more weight than new "Archivists".

## 3. 🏆 Gamified Rewards
- **Predictability Score (PS)**: Your global rank is determined by how closely your predictions align with eventual outcomes.
- **Digital Badges**: Earn 8 unique badges (e.g., "Full Coverage", "District Master") to display on your profile.
- **Influence Points**: Gain points for every prediction made, scaling with your confidence.

## 4. 📲 Authentication 2.0
- **Phone OTP**: High-security registration requiring a valid phone number.
- **Google Login**: One-click onboarding.
- **Dual Session Cleanup**: Automated logout logic to prevent session contamination.

## 5. 🔔 Real-time Notifications
- Get alerted instantly when you earn a badge or when a new election phase is announced.
- All actions are logged for your personal archive.
